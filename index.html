01 / Company

What is Arima Finance?

Arima Finance (AF) is an independent quantitative finance and financial technology company focused on building intelligent solutions for financial markets.

We combine quantitative research, artificial intelligence, software engineering and financial expertise to develop products for traders, investors, institutions and financial professionals. Our mission is to bridge technology and finance through innovation.

About

Aryan Heidari

Finance professional and fintech builder, based in London.

Aryan works at the intersection of financial analysis and technical systems — researching markets, building financial models, and developing the software that turns that analysis into structured, auditable decision pipelines.

His research background spans artificial intelligence in finance, blockchain infrastructure and decentralised finance, published through SSRN and ResearchGate. Alongside research, he advises client portfolios through Arima Finance across options, equity and commodity strategies, and has contributed financial-modelling and information-memorandum work in a boutique investment-banking advisory setting.

That combination — analysis, research and engineering — is what led to Arima Finance Engine: a shift from producing one-off analysis toward building reusable, testable market-analysis infrastructure. He holds an MSc in Finance from Anglia Ruskin University and is a CFA Level III candidate. He previously co-founded a Web3 venture, AEXT Ltd, since exited.

Long-term, his focus stays on market technology and financial research: tools and analysis that hold up to scrutiny, not just to a first read.
